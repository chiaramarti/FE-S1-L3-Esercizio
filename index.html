Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

image: Sally Vegas

Featured in: Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

    For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. 1. Make the focaccia dough In a medium-sized mixing bol, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
